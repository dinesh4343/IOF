Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how our bank exam coaching center collects, uses, discloses, and protects the personal information of our students and visitors. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and confidentiality of all personal information shared with us. By enrolling in our coaching center or using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection:

a. Personal Information: We may collect personal information, including but not limited to, your name, contact details, date of birth, educational background, and payment information when you enroll in our coaching program or use our services.
b. Non-Personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical details when you visit our website.

Use of Information:

a. We use the collected personal information to provide our coaching services, including enrollment management, academic support, and communication regarding course updates.
b. Non-personal information is used to improve our website's functionality, analyze trends, and enhance user experience.

Information Sharing:

a. We may share personal information with our authorized staff and faculty members for the purpose of delivering our coaching services.
b. We may share personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our coaching center, such as payment processors or IT support services. These providers are contractually bound to maintain the confidentiality and security of personal information.
c. We may disclose personal information if required by law, legal process, or government request.

Security:

a. We implement reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
b. However, no data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Retention of Information:

a. We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
b. Non-personal information may be retained indefinitely for statistical and analytical purposes.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

a. Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze trends, and gather information about website usage.
b. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit certain features and functionality of our website.

Third-Party Links:

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those websites before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy:

Our coaching center and website are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.

Updates to the Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the revised policy on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
