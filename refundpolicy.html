The payment made for the coaching program is non-transferable to any other individual or course. It is solely for the specific program you have enrolled in.

We do not offer partial refunds for any portion of the coaching program, including missed classes, unused study materials, or any other services provided as part of the program.

We understand that circumstances can change; however, requests for refunds due to a change of mind or personal circumstances will not be entertained.
